About Me

The Story So Far

I'm a third-year Computer Science student at the University of the West Indies (St. Augustine), with a focus on backend systems, data-driven applications, and low-level computing.

My work lives at the intersection of scalable backend architecture and systems-level thinking — whether that's designing multi-strategy scheduling engines, building relational database schemas, or experimenting with unconventional storage solutions.

I'm driven by curiosity for how software truly works beneath the surface — from OS process management to TCP/UDP networking. When I'm not coding, I'm exploring new system architectures and thinking about how things scale.
